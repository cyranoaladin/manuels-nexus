\textbf{Question 3.} $S_{2^p} = 1 + \dfrac{1}{2} + \left(\dfrac{1}{3}+\dfrac{1}{4}\right) + \cdots \geq 1 + \dfrac{1}{2} + \dfrac{2}{4} + \dfrac{4}{8} + \cdots = 1 + \dfrac{p}{2}$.
