\begin{corrige}{TSPE-SUITLIM-EX-018}

\textbf{Question 1.} $0{,}6$ : $40\,\%$ de la population disparait (mortalite/migration). $120$ : apport annuel (naissances/repeuplements).

\textbf{Question 2.} $\ell = 0{,}6\ell + 120 \Rightarrow \ell = 300$.

\textbf{Question 3.} $v_n = u_n - 300$, $v_0 = -250$, $v_n = -250 \times 0{,}6^n$. $u_n = 300 - 250 \times 0{,}6^n$.

\textbf{Question 4.} $u_{n+1} - u_n = 0{,}6\,u_n + 120 - u_n = 120 - 0{,}4\,u_n$. Or $u_n < 300$ (car $250 \times 0{,}6^n > 0$), donc $u_{n+1} - u_n > 120 - 120 = 0$. Croissante. $\lim u_n = \boxed{300}$ centaines.
