\medskip
\textbf{2.} $P_C(F) = \dfrac{P(C \cap F)}{P(C)} = \dfrac{\frac{3}{32}}{\frac{1}{4}} = \dfrac{3}{32} \times 4 = \dfrac{3}{8}$.
