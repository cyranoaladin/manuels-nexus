\begin{corrige}{1SPE-PROBCOND-EX-029}

Par probabilites totales : $P(A) = P(B)\,P_B(A) + P(\overline{B})\,P_{\overline{B}}(A)$.

$\frac{3}{10} = p \times \frac{1}{2} + (1-p) \times \frac{1}{5} = \frac{p}{2} + \frac{1}{5} - \frac{p}{5} = \frac{3p}{10} + \frac{1}{5}$.

$\frac{3}{10} - \frac{1}{5} = \frac{3p}{10}$, donc $\frac{1}{10} = \frac{3p}{10}$, d'ou $p = \frac{1}{3}$.

\end{corrige}
