\commentaireMarge{Factoriser par le terme dominant.}

\textbf{1.} $\dfrac{5x-3}{2x+7} = \dfrac{5-3/x}{2+7/x} \to \boxed{\dfrac{5}{2}}$.

\textbf{2.} $\dfrac{x^2+1}{3x^2-x} = \dfrac{1+1/x^2}{3-1/x} \to \boxed{\dfrac{1}{3}}$.
