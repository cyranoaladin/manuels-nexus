\begin{corrige}{1SPE-PRODSCAL-EX-032}
$\overrightarrow{BC}\begin{pmatrix} -4 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix}$. On parametrise : $H = B + t\,\overrightarrow{BC} = (6 - 4t; 4t)$.

$\overrightarrow{AH} = \begin{pmatrix} 6-4t \\ 4t \end{pmatrix}$. La condition $\overrightarrow{AH} \cdot \overrightarrow{BC} = 0$ donne :
$(6-4t)(-4) + 4t \times 4 = 0$, soit $-24 + 16t + 16t = 0$, d'ou $t = \dfrac{3}{4}$.

$H = \left(3; 3\right)$.
