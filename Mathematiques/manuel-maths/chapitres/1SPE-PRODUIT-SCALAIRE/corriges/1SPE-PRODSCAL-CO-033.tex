$\overrightarrow{AB} \cdot \overrightarrow{AC} = 4$, $\|\overrightarrow{AB}\| = 4$, $\|\overrightarrow{AC}\| = \sqrt{10}$.

$\cos\widehat{A} = \dfrac{4}{4\sqrt{10}} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{10}}$, $\sin\widehat{A} = \dfrac{3}{\sqrt{10}}$.
