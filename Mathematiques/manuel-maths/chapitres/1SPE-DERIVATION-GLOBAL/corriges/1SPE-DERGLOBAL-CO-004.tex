On applique la linéarité de la dérivation $(f+g)' = f' + g'$.

\textbf{1.} $f'(x) = 3x^2 + 2x$.

\textbf{2.} $g'(x) = 4x^3 - \left(-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right) = 4x^3 + \dfrac{1}{x^2}$.

\textbf{3.} $h'(x) = \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}} + 1$.
