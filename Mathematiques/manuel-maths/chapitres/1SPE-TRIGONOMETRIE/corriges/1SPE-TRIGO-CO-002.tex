\commentaireMarge{Multiplier par $\frac{180}{\pi}$.}

\textbf{1.} $\dfrac{\pi}{3} = \dfrac{\pi}{3} \times \dfrac{180}{\pi} = \dfrac{180}{3} = 60°$.

\textbf{2.} $\dfrac{5\pi}{4} = \dfrac{5\pi}{4} \times \dfrac{180}{\pi} = \dfrac{5 \times 180}{4} = 225°$.

\textbf{3.} $\dfrac{7\pi}{6} = \dfrac{7\pi}{6} \times \dfrac{180}{\pi} = \dfrac{7 \times 180}{6} = 210°$.

\textbf{4.} $\dfrac{2\pi}{9} = \dfrac{2\pi}{9} \times \dfrac{180}{\pi} = \dfrac{2 \times 180}{9} = 40°$.
